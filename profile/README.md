## â“ FAQ

### What is a gas fee?
A gas fee is the cost required to process a transaction or smart contract interaction on a blockchain network.

### Why do gas fees change?
Gas fees fluctuate based on network demand, congestion, and block usage.

### Is this an on-chain tool?
No. This calculator operates **off-chain** and does not submit transactions.

### Can I use this for DeFi or NFT transactions?
Yes. It is suitable for estimating costs for swaps, mints, transfers, and contract interactions.
